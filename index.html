Our goal is the creation of a user empowered personal health record that is verifiable and secure at any point of care.

Challenges in today's healthcare.

Today's healthcare faces a number challenges. This provides an windows in which we can explore a new range of technologies and methods to tackle these unique challenges.

Fragmented

Personal Health information is silo’d and lacks interoperability.

Transferability

Different methods of tracking a patient’s history, making it difficult to share at different points of care.

Reliability

No single solution that can provide authenticated patient’s data, while protecting patient privacy.
